Our Family Life
Changed Forever

The offensive of the army of the Russian Federation on Ukraine began simultaneously from several directions.

One of the key directions for the capture of Kyiv was the North, from the side of the Republic of Belarus.

Cities such as Borodyanka, Gostomel, Bucha and Irpin were subjected to massive air strikes from the first day of the offensive.

ABOUT

Hi! I'm Aleksandra

My name is Alexandra! I am a mother of two children, a boy George and a girl Dasha, who miraculously escaped from the city called Irpin.

On February 24, 2022, Russia invaded Ukraine and our city was one of the first to take on air and artillery strikes.

  • 2 Shells Hit
    The House

    After spending the first day in the basement of a neighboring house, next morning February 25, it was decided to evacuate to western Ukraine and then to Europe.

    For about a month, fierce battles were fought for our beloved city, which moved into the most active phase on March 23.

  • 3 Days The
    Fire Lasted

    After the arrival of several shells in a residential complex, our apartment caught fire. According to eyewitnesses, the apartment was burning for 2 days!

    The tense situation and constant shelling did not allow public utilities to quickly eliminate the fire. Everything acquired over 10 years of a happy life was completely destroyed in an instant!

  • Neighbor House
    Half Destroyed

    The current situation of my family is tough as my husband and I have lost our jobs, and we are struggling to make ends meet. We are doing everything we can to earn some money for our family's basic needs, but we are finding it challenging to restore our damaged apartment on our own. The thought of renovating our home anytime soon seems impractical due to our financial constraints.

    Any contribution towards our household would help us return to our hometown and begin the restoration process of our cherished, small apartment. It holds a special place in our hearts as it has been a witness to our joy and happiness before the war. We would be immensely grateful for any support that could assist us in rebuilding our home.

  • Apartment
    Burned Down

    As we sit in the family circle and remember cherished possessions that are now lost, tears stream down our faces. For our children, the loss of their favorite toys and personal items, some of which have been with them since infancy, is especially difficult to bear.

    It can be challenging to let go of things that hold sentimental value, but we hold onto the memories and the love that these objects represent. Though the pain may linger, we find comfort in knowing that we still have each other, and that our love and bond as a family will endure.

    We thank God for saving our lives, the lives of our children and relatives! We really hope that Ukraine will survive!
